Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How to choose a suitable capacitor?

You should check the capacitance value (µF / nF), the maximum working voltage, and the capacitor type (ceramic, electrolytic, tantalum, etc.).

What is the difference between a regular diode and a Zener diode?

A regular diode allows current to flow in one direction only, while a Zener diode is designed for voltage regulation and operates in controlled breakdown.

How do I know which resistor I need?

You should calculate the resistance value using Ohm’s Law (V = IR) and also check the resistor’s power rating (W).

Are the components suitable for educational projects?

Yes. All components on the site are suitable for learning, experiments, school assignments, and projects in schools and colleges.
